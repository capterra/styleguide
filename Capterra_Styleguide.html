    Stacks

    Used for horizontal layout stacks which span the full width of the browser.

    Use .stack-content following your .stack div to center content and add top & bottom padding. There are 3 stack-content options:

    • stack-content: adds 24px padding to top & bottom of stack
    • stack-content-thick: adds 48px padding to top & bottom of stack
    • stack-content-fat: adds 72px padding to top & 48px padding to bottom of stack

    Grid System

    Our grid system is composed of 12 flexible columns with optional varying gutter sizes. Append your specified column width next to .cell.

    Grids always wrap around cells. In other words, cells must always follow grids.

    Gutter options (use .grid as your base):

    • grid - 24px margins
    • grid-thin - 12px margins
    • grid-thick - 36px margins
    • grid-fat - 48px margins
